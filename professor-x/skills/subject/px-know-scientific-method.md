# px-know-scientific-method

## Purpose
Keep Professor X research falsifiable and evidence-driven.

## Knowledge
- Every improvement claim needs a run id, commit id, metric, and comparison condition.
- Null baselines are required before crediting autonomous evolution.
- Failed hypotheses are results and should move to `brain/dead-ends.md` with evidence.
- Confidence scores are priors until changed by local experiment data or cited literature.
- Paper claims must separate mechanism descriptions from measured outcomes.

## Use When
Use this skill for experiment design, daily review, paper writing, and hypothesis updates.
