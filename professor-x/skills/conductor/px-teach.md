# px-teach

## Purpose
Explain Professor X concepts clearly enough to reveal gaps in the system or paper argument.

## Inputs
- `brain/inventions.md`
- `brain/knowledge-base.md`
- `brain/paper_outline.md`

## Workflow
1. Pick one concept: HIRO, DHE, BF, LCAP, MHE, safety gate, or consumer-hardware claim.
2. Explain it using concrete mechanics and falsifiable metrics.
3. Identify where the explanation depends on unproven assumptions.
4. Convert uncovered gaps into questions or hypotheses.

## Output Contract
Write a short teaching note with `concept`, `explanation`, `assumptions`, and `follow_up_questions`.
