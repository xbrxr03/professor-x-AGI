# Falsification Checklist

Every surviving invention candidate needs a kill path.

## Questions

- What empirical result would disconfirm the claim?
- What simpler baseline could explain the same outcome?
- What would show the proposal is only a metaphor?
- What hidden assumption is carrying the whole theory?
- What part depends on current benchmark design?
- What would fail under distribution shift or adversarial evaluation?

## Theory hygiene

Separate:

- descriptive theory
- engineering architecture
- evaluation method
- product strategy

Do not let one masquerade as another.

## Minimum kill package

Before promoting a candidate, define:

- one baseline
- one adverse case
- one ablation
- one no-effect result that would weaken the theory
