# Experiment Results

## Temperature Comparison (0.5 vs 1.0)

| Metric          | Temperature 0.5 | Temperature 1.0 | p-value |
|----------------|----------------|----------------|---------|
| Creativity      | 8.2 (±1.1)     | 6.7 (±1.5)     | 0.003*  |
| Coherence       | 9.1 (±0.8)     | 8.9 (±0.9)     | 0.12    |

*Significant at p<0.05

## Conclusion
Higher temperature (0.5) significantly improves creativity while maintaining coherence (p=0.003).
