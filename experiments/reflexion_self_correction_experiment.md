## Reflexion Self-Correction Experiment

### Objective
Measure whether Reflexion (verbal self-reflection) improves success rate on the HIRO self-correction category.

### Steps
1. **Baseline Measurement**: Run 50 trials without Reflexion, recording success rates.
2. **Reflexion Integration**: Run 50 trials with Reflexion, recording success rates.
3. **Comparison**: Compare the success rates from the two sets of trials to determine if Reflexion improves performance.

### What to Measure
- Number of successful trials in each set.
- Time taken per trial.
- Quality of self-reflection in Reflexion trials.

### What Constitutes a Positive Result
- Reflexion trials show a statistically significant improvement in success rate compared to baseline trials.
- Reflexion trials demonstrate improved self-reflection quality and faster resolution of errors.
