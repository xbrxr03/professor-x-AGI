## Reflexion Experiment Plan

### Objective:
Measure whether Reflexion (verbal self-reflection) improves success rate on the HIRO self-correction category.

### Hypothesis:
Reflexion improves the success rate of the HIRO self-correction category.

### Experimental Design:
1. **Participants:** Two groups of agents, one using Reflexion and the other not.
2. **Tasks:** HIRO self-correction tasks.
3. **Trials:** Each agent will perform 100 trials.
4. **Success Metric:** The number of successful self-corrections.

### Expected Outcome:
If Reflexion improves success rate, the group using Reflexion will have a higher success rate than the group without Reflexion.

### Analysis:
- Compare the success rates of the two groups using statistical analysis.
- Determine if the difference is statistically significant.

### Conclusion:
This experiment will determine whether Reflexion improves the success rate of the HIRO self-correction category.
